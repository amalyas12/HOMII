How to Use Homi App ?

Posted by Nadjib Mohammed SALMI July 20, 2020

Connect with your account and connect the app with bluetooth You’ll see the name of your home at the top of the screen. If you have multiple homes set up, you can tap your home name to switch between homes. Quick Actions Here you have general controls over your whole home. Each button acts as a control for a device, service or group of devices and services. Each has a different function depending on which device it represents and the state that device is in. Lights off: Turn off your smart lights by room, or turn off all smart lights in the home. Lights on: Turn on your smart lights by room, or turn on all smart lights in the home. Media: Shows media you currently have playing and which device it’s playing on. If you have more than one device, you can choose to stream from one device to another. Routines: Create and manage routines for your device.
